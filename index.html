Find the perfect roommate.

With Seek, your roommate search is quick, easy, and enjoyable. You'll never have to scroll through long lists of anonymous ads on Craigslist again! Welcome to the new standard.

Available for iOS. Android coming soon.

Real friends of real people.

Seek requires you to log in with Facebook to verify your identity. It also allows us to show any mutual friends you may have with potential roommates!

Place, personality, and preferences.

Using the information you provide on where you want to live, who you are, and what you're looking for, allows us to provide a match percentage with potential roommates. You can use it to quickly make a decision on which way to swipe!

Spam-free connections.

Sifting through hundreds of email responses to a Craigslist post is no way to find a roommate. With Seek, only people you've matched with can message you.
